Hiphop workshop

HOUR AND DATE

26. June 10:00-12:00

PLACE

The mirror room in the basement

Do you want to learn and dance the steps you see on youtube or just learn and dance so you can show yourself ahead of the others then take this workshop with Fredrik Bauner. Fredrik has on several occasions represented Denmark at both the European and World Championships in hip hop and Electric Boogie, and has several super nice locations behind him - in 2012 Fredrik became world champion in Electric Boogie. Fredrik has also danced in various TV shows such as the Danish Music Awards. Fredrik loves to dance, and it obviously rubs off in his teaching, which is always full of fun and cool choreographies. Come and have a party with him and then he promises you that he gives everything he has. We are seen at a world-class workshop.

Teacher: Fredrik Bauner, former student at Haslev Private School
