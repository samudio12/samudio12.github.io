DSB School trips

School trips are booked through DSB at www.dsb.dk. It is important to remember that tickets must be ordered at least 71 days (10 weeks) before the trip and that the order must be done in class. At dsb.dk you can see how you order DSB School Travel, but remember to have the school's UVM's school number, the school's phone number, and the mobile number of the contact during the journey ready before you start booking online. Also, remember that you will receive an email with a link that you need to click to confirm the order. The order will be canceled after 4 days if you have not clicked on the link in the email. As school trips through DSB only apply to train journeys, there is a bus ready to drive you to and from Aalborg Friskole from the train station.
