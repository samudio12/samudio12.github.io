Water gun competition

Join us in a Water-gun fight to cool off.
In this workshop we will have 8 teams of four people.
Everyone will get a „water reactive” paper what turns red (from blue) if it gets wet enough. Make teams and show off your skills in the woods/field. One match is approx. 10 minutes long and 2 team goes head to head, last one standing wins. At the end the best 2 team will have a showdown for the grand prize (yet to be announced).
We will provide the water-guns on the spot, but you can bring your own if you feel like it.
Sign up now, before the spots fill up. 36people in total.
