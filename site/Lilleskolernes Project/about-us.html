About Us

A unique universe with over 1,700 students playing music and theater for 3 days in June. The Little School Festival is a completely unique teaching, learning and children's culture, which teachers and students have made something special for almost 40 years.

The Little Schools Music and Theater Festival is an annual event for pupils from the country's schoolchildren. It is a creative festival where music, dance, theater, art and socializing are the focal point. The festival will be held in turn by Lilleskoler around the country, and it is supported by the Lilleskoles association. The organizers' school teachers are in collaboration with the school's parents for the planning and conducting of the festival. It is a huge work carried by committed parents and teachers who are passionate about creating a framework for creative expression and socializing.

Workshop registration is open Wednesday, June 20, 2018, at. 10:00.
