Green house

We are all about nature.
In this workshop we will learn about all about climate change, plastic in oceans and the vanishing of the jungles.
This is not just a presentation that what is bad in the world and what should be doing to change it. We will do an interactive quiz to begin the whole workshop, than move on to the 21st Century and Social Media about the Climate Change, how big Brands like Adidas are also helping cleaning the oceans.
To top off the workshop we will also do the „Plastic Free Challenge” in two teams.
The winner team will be given re-usable bags and hats.
Sign up now, before the spots fill up completely. 75-80 people in total.
